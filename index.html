The Sky Over RedCloud

Long ago, the sky above RedCloud was quiet. No drifting orbs, no falling creatures, only a faded glow on the horizon. Then the first shard of light split the clouds, and the world began to wake.

The orbs were the first to descend—curious lights that guided wanderers toward forgotten places. Soon after came gentle caretakers like Sprog, keeping watch over new arrivals and lost relics. Now, with each new game and tiny world you visit, more of RedCloud's story comes into focus. Every creature you meet is another piece of the sky, stitched back together.
